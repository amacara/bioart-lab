Bioart Curriculum

Open-source lessons, recipes, and project structures for experimental biology, bioart, biomaterials, fungi, and bioelectronics.

teaching areas

hands-on science through living materials

Biomaterials

Recipes and lessons for bioplastics, bioyarn, biopastes, mycelium materials, and other biodegradable material experiments.

Biochromes

Activities exploring natural pigments, fungal dyes, bacterial color, plant pigments, chromatography, and color as biological process.

Bioelectronics

Projects connecting circuits, sensors, LEDs, sound, feedback, and biological signals through creative electronics.

Microscopy / Optics

Lessons exploring magnification, lenses, microscopy, and tools for observing biological systems through hands-on fabrication and experimentation.

Living BioLibrary

A semester-long student-driven curriculum exploring biomaterials, biochromes, and bioelectronics through research, material experimentation, and creative bioart project development.
